⚠️ Education, Not Financial Advice

TradeGame uses entirely fictional instruments and simulated markets. This is an educational project. Nothing here constitutes a signal or trade recommendation. Always consult a licensed professional before trading with real money.

🎯 Scenario Replays

Step inside famous market moments tick-by-tick, make your decisions, then get the debrief on trading process and risk lessons.

🛠 Strategy Sandboxes

Build a crypto grid-bot visually. Simulate DCA/rebalancing. See where grid strategies break — before real money is at stake.

🛡 Risk-Management Drills

Position-sizing puzzles, drawdown survival challenges, even a "blow up the account on purpose" teaching mode. Safe, simulated, real lessons.

📊 Process-First Progression

Advancement rewards correct sizing, honoring stops, and journaling — not profit. Good process with bad outcomes is celebrated as a lesson.
