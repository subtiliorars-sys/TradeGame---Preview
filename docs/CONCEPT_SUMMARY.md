# TradeGame — Concept Summary (public)

## The idea

Gamers already know how to get good at hard things: practice modes, replays,
drills, ranked ladders, coaching. Trading is a hard thing that most people are
taught badly — usually by someone selling them signals. TradeGame applies the
gamer learning loop to trading instead.

## Three equal market pillars

Every part of TradeGame treats three markets as first-class:

| Pillar | Example topics |
|---|---|
| **Crypto** | spot, day trading, grid strategies, liquidity pools, AI×crypto, crypto in games |
| **Stocks** | long-term investing, ETFs, swing trading, earnings seasons |
| **Forex** | major pairs, trading sessions, and above all: risk management & leverage danger |

No pillar is a bolt-on. Channels, lessons, game scenarios, and content rotate
across all three.

## The game

An education video game — a trading-practice simulator:

- **Paper-trading sandboxes** per market. Simulated money only.
- **Scenario replays** — step into historical market moments tick-by-tick,
  make your decisions, then get the debrief on what actually happened and why.
- **Strategy sandboxes** — e.g. a visual crypto grid-bot builder that teaches
  where grid strategies break; DCA/rebalancing sims for stocks; session-timing
  sims for forex.
- **Risk-management drills** — position-sizing puzzles, drawdown survival
  challenges, even a "blow up the account on purpose" teaching mode.
- **Progression that rewards process** — XP comes from sizing correctly,
  honoring stops, and journaling — not from lucky PnL. Good process with a bad
  outcome is celebrated as a lesson, not hidden.

## The community

A Discord-centered, community-run organization:

- Learn / analysis / strategy / **wins-AND-losses journal** channels per pillar.
- A coaching ladder — learner → helper → coach — earned through the game's
  progression. Coaching covers process, risk, and journaling.
- Replay-review sessions: coaches annotate your sim trades like a game coach
  reviews your VOD.

## The lines we don't cross

- **Education, not financial advice.** Nobody here tells you what to buy or sell.
- **No signal-selling, ever.** It's the scam pattern we define ourselves against.
- **No real money in the game.** Simulation only.
- **No gambling mechanics.** No loot boxes, no wager loops.

## Status

Early concept stage — community design and game design in progress.
