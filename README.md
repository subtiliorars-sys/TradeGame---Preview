# TradeGame

**Learn to trade the way gamers learn games: practice, replays, and ranked progression — with zero real money on the line.**

TradeGame is a community-run coaching and gaming organization for video gamers
who trade, and an education video game in the making.

## What it is

- **A community** — a Discord-centered home for gamer-traders, with three
  equal market pillars: **crypto, stocks, and forex**. Learn-by-doing channels,
  wins-and-losses journals, peer cohorts, and member coaches.
- **A game** — a trading-practice simulator: paper-trading sandboxes, historical
  scenario replays (trade inside famous market moments, then get the debrief),
  strategy sandboxes like a crypto grid-bot builder, and risk-management drills.
  Progression rewards good process — position sizing, stops, journaling — not
  just profit.
- **A coaching ladder** — learner → helper → coach. Coaching covers process,
  risk management, and trade journaling. Entry is earned through the game's
  progression, not bought.

## What it is not

- ❌ **Not financial advice.** TradeGame is education only.
- ❌ **No signals.** No buy/sell calls, no "VIP signal" tiers — ever. The
  community is structurally designed so signal-selling can't exist here.
- ❌ **No real money.** The game simulates markets; it never touches a real
  brokerage or exchange account.
- ❌ **No lambo culture.** Losses are part of the curriculum; we journal them.
